## 💖 About
Cutekind is about the little things—  
a smile 😊, a kind word 💬, a small act of help 🤝  

They may seem simple, but they can make someone’s day brighter  
and the world a little more beautiful.

## ✨ The Idea
Sometimes it’s just a small moment—  
telling someone their shirt looks nice, helping without being asked,  
or simply smiling at someone.

These moments feel small…  
but they can mean everything.

Because small actions can create big happiness.
